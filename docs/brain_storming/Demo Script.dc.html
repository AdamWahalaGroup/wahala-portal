Questions you will get — answers ready

  • "Do they pay the deposit AND Stage 1?" No — the deposit IS Stage 1's payment in full. Stage 1 opens Paid; Stages 2–3 pay-gate as usual. (It's now written on frame 34.)
  • "Is payment live?" The pay-gate is enforced today; checkout is admin "Mark paid" until Stripe ships. Frame 08 is the target UI.
  • "Can we delete a client?" No — archive only (frame 14b). Records are forever; that's the audit story we sell.
  • "Why do deals and projects both say 'stage'?" They don't anymore: deals move through pipeline steps; projects have paid Stages.
  • "What if a client goes silent?" SLA nudges + the at-risk strip + the stuck pill (frames 28 / 31); proposal follow-up defaults to 7 days.
  • "Change orders bypass Stripe?" Interim, yes — admin marks paid. Client card-payment for change orders lands with milestone billing.
