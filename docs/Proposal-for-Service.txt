Executive Summary


Talden has successfully validated a compelling product vision centered around AI-assisted legal work product creation, review, and research. The existing platform demonstrates functional workflows and establishes a strong foundation for commercialization.


Following a comprehensive technical and architectural assessment, it has been determined that the platform has progressed beyond the concept stage but requires additional engineering to meet the standards expected of a secure, scalable, commercially deployable SaaS product.


This proposal defines a fixed-price engagement to complete the engineering work necessary to transition Talden from an advanced prototype into a production-ready commercial platform. The work will be delivered in three structured phases, each with clearly defined scope, deliverables, acceptance criteria, and payment milestones.


The total fixed investment for this engagement is $225,000, delivered over approximately 28 weeks.


---


Scope of Engagement


Wahala Group will provide software engineering, architecture, implementation, testing, and deployment services required to commercialize the Talden platform.


The engagement includes application development, cloud infrastructure, security hardening, quality assurance, deployment support, and technical documentation necessary to achieve commercial readiness.


This proposal excludes ongoing operational support, feature development outside the agreed scope, marketing activities, and production operations unless specifically authorized through a written change order.

Statement of Work


Phase 1 – Private Beta Foundation


Duration: 8 Weeks


Fixed Price: $65,000


Objective


Build the foundational platform capabilities required to support secure multi-tenant private beta customers.


Scope of Work


The Contractor shall design, develop, configure, test, and deploy the following functionality:


Authentication & Identity


- User registration
- Login and authentication
- Password reset
- Session management
- Role-based access control


Organization & Tenant Management


- Multi-tenant architecture
- Organization management
- Workspace isolation
- User invitations
- Membership management
- Permission framework


##Matter Persistence


- Persistent matter storage
- Workspace persistence
- Document persistence
- Metadata management


##Audit & Activity Logging


- User activity history
- Matter history
- Administrative audit logs
- Prompt history
- Document revision history


##Administrative Controls


- User administration
- Organization administration
- Feature controls
- Environment configuration


##Usage Tracking


- AI usage metering
- Cost tracking
- Operational analytics


##Security Hardening


- Secrets management
- API security
- Encryption validation
- Dependency review
- Logging improvements


##Deliverables


- Production authentication
- Multi-tenant platform
- Persistent workspaces
- Audit logging
- Administrative dashboard
- Usage reporting
- Security improvements


##Acceptance Criteria
